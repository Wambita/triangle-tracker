Types Of Triangles

Equilateral Triangle

An equilateral triangle is a triangle whose all three sides are equal and whose all interior angles are equal and each is equal to 60 degrees

Isoceles Triangle

Isoceles triangle is a triangle whose any two sides are equal and its base anglesare equal

Scalene Triangle

A triangle with all sides of different lengths and All angles are different, too.
